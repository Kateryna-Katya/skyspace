Core Concept

Strength Is Earned,
Not Given

The game unfolds through a symbol-driven system where hostile entities emerge and evolve.
Certain symbols appear above the main field, influencing what exists beneath them.

When aligned correctly, these forces can be neutralized — transformed into allies that reshape the field and open new paths forward. Every action matters.
Every sequence changes what comes next. This is not about speed. It is about reading the signs.

Wild Attack

Corruption Shift

Special influence symbols may appear on the upper layer of the field.
When such a symbol aligns directly above a powerful hostile entity, the entity is purified and transformed.
After the transformation, new combinations become possible, altering the entire flow of the round.
These transformed symbols can adapt to most other elements on the field, making them a crucial tool for survival.

Ritual Symbols

Ritual Marks

When multiple Ritual Marks appear together, the game shifts into a heightened state of challenge.

Depending on how many marks are revealed at once, the duration of this intensified phase will vary,
extending the confrontation and increasing the pressure.
This phase is not a reward. It is a test.

Intensified Phase

Trial of the Deep

During the Trial of the Deep, each hostile entity is tracked individually. A counter shows how many remain before the entity is fully subdued. When a purification effect occurs during this phase, the remaining count is reduced. Once an entity is completely overcome, it no longer returns in its original form — instead, it becomes a permanent empowered symbol for the rest of the trial. By the end, the field is no longer what it was at the start.
