The World

The Altar
That Should Not Be Touched

So we've got this ancient altar thing - whoever built it clearly knew that power comes with a price.
What looks like a portal is, in fact, a barrier — holding something back.
Stone guardians were raised to keep the balance. They still stand. But they are weakening.
Those who enter this world do not chase easy victories. They endure. They resist.
They learn the rules written long before them.

Core Concept

Strength Is Earned,
Not Given

The game unfolds through a symbol-driven system where hostile entities emerge and evolve.
Certain symbols appear above the main field, influencing what exists beneath them.

When aligned correctly, these forces can be neutralized — transformed into allies that reshape the field and open new paths forward. Every action matters.
Every sequence changes what comes next. This is not about speed. It is about reading the signs.
